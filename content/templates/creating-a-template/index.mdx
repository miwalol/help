---
title: Creating a Template
description: Learn how to create and share your own Miwa.lol profile template, including its preview, name, tags, and privacy.
---

**To create a template, go in your Dashboard > [Templates](https://miwa.lol/dashboard/templates),** then click on the *Create Template* button.

## Options

- **Preview:** A preview of the template. We recommend taking a screenshot of your page.
- **Name:** The template name. Can be anything you want.
- **Tags:** A list of tags that people can search for to find your template.
- **Private:** Do you want your template to be findable/used by anyone?
