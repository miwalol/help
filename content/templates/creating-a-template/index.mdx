---
title: Creating a Template
description: Learn how to create and share your own Miwa.lol profile template, including its preview, name, tags, and privacy.
---

**To create a template, go to your Dashboard > [Templates](https://miwa.lol/dashboard/templates),** then click the *Create Template* button.

## Options

- **Preview:** A preview of the template. We recommend taking a screenshot of your page.
- **Name:** The template name. Can be anything you want.
- **Tags:** A list of tags that people can search for to find your template.
- **Visibility:** Controls who can find and use your template:
  - **Public:** Listed and searchable by anyone in the public templates list.
  - **Unlisted:** Hidden from the list and search, but anyone with the link can use it.
  - **Private:** Only you can see and use it.
