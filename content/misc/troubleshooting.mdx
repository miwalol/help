### I don't receive the verification email

If you don't receive the verification email, please check your spam folder. Keep in mind that some email providers, such as Gmail, may block our emails or delay them.

If you still don't receive the email, please [contact us](/misc/contact) for assistance.

### I forgot my password

If you forgot your password, you can reset it by clicking on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You will need to provide your email address, and we will send you a password reset link.

### I got banned

When you got banned, we emailed you, explaining the reason for your ban. If you believe this was a mistake, please [contact us](/misc/contact) for assistance.

Also, make sure to read our [Terms of Service](https://miwa.lol/terms) to understand what behavior is not allowed on Miwa.lol. We take violations of our Terms of Service seriously and will not hesitate to ban users who violate them. Don't hesitate to read this page:

### How can I report a profile?

If you come across a profile that you believe violates our [Terms of Service](https://miwa.lol/terms), you can report it by following these steps:

<Steps>
  1. Go to the profile you want to report.

  2. Click on the <Flag /> icon in the top right corner of the profile page.

  3. Select the reason for your report from the choices provided, and provide any additional information if necessary.

  4. Click on the "**Submit**" button to send your report.

  5. Our team will review the report and take appropriate action if necessary.
</Steps>

Thank you for helping us keep Miwa.lol a safe and enjoyable place for everyone!
