**This feature allows you to tweak the look and feel of your cards to better match your personal style or branding, by enabling or disabling certain elements on your cards.**

Each card type has its own settings that you can customize.
To access the settings for a specific card type, go to the **[Cards](https://miwa.lol/dashboard/cards)** page in the sidebar, and then click on the <Settings /> icon next to the card type you want to customize.
If you don't see the settings icon, it means that the card type doesn't have any customizable settings.
