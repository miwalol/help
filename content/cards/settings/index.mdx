**Card settings let you tweak the look and feel of your cards to better match your personal style or branding, by enabling or disabling individual elements.**

Each card type has its own set of settings, and some card types have none at all.

## Accessing card settings

Card settings live inside the card editor, right where you build the card:

- **When creating a card:** on the **[Cards](https://miwa.lol/dashboard/cards)** page, click **Create**, choose a platform, then scroll down to the **Settings** section.
- **When editing a card:** on the **[Cards](https://miwa.lol/dashboard/cards)** page, click the <Pencil /> icon on the card you want to change, then scroll down to the **Settings** section.

If a card doesn't show a **Settings** section, that card type simply has no options to customize.

## Settings by card type

The available settings vary depending on the card. These are the card types that can be customized:

| Card                 | Available settings                                                         |
|----------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Discord Presence** | Avatar decoration, badges, server tag                                      |
| **Twitter/X**        | Followers count, tweets count, location, join date *(off by default)*, bio |
| **YouTube**          | Subscribers count, videos count                                            |
| **Minecraft**        | Show capes, and which cape providers to pull from<sup>1</sup>              |
| **AniList**          | Show profile<sup>2</sup>                                                   |
| **MyAnimeList**      | Show profile<sup>2</sup>                                                   |
| **Last.fm**          | Show profile<sup>2</sup>, recent tracks<sup>3</sup>                        |
| **Xbox**             | Show profile<sup>2</sup>, recent games<sup>3</sup>                         |
| **Audio player**     | Show queue<sup>3</sup> *(off by default)*                                  |
| **Location**         | Satellite view *(off by default)*                                          |

<sup>1</sup> Once *Show capes* is on you can pick which providers to source capes from: Minecraft, OptiFine, MinecraftCapes, LabyMod, 5zig, TLauncher, and SkinMC. All are enabled by default.

<sup>2</sup> *Profile* here means the header block: your avatar, username, and stats. Turning it off hands the card's space to its other content instead (recent activity, tracks, or games).

<sup>3</sup> Requires a taller card, see [below](#settings-that-need-a-taller-card).

## Settings that need a taller card

Some settings stay greyed out until the card is tall enough to fit the extra content. The Last.fm **recent tracks**, Xbox **recent games**, and Audio player **queue** toggles only become available once the card is at least **2 rows** tall - or, for Last.fm and Xbox, once you hide the profile so the list has room.

You can change the number of rows in the [Card rows](/cards/creating-a-card/#card-rows) section of the editor, which is also a Premium feature.
