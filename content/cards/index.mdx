**Cards are a great way to showcase your content, such as your Discord server, a Spotify track, a GitHub repository, and more.** You can add cards to your profile page to display information about yourself or your interests.

You can add up to 2 cards to your page, or up to 6 if you have a Premium account.

<Aside type="info">
  To follow this guide, go to your [dashboard](https://miwa.lol/dashboard) and click on the [Cards](https://miwa.lol/dashboard/cards) page in the sidebar.
</Aside>

## Creating a Card

To add a card to your page, click the ***Create a card*** button on the right side of the page.
Then choose the type of card you want to add:

- **Discord presence**: Show your Discord status and activity. *You'll need to [link your Discord account](/how-to/link-your-discord/) to use this card.*
- **Discord server**: Display server info, join button, and member stats
- **Spotify embed**: Embed a track, artist, album, or playlist
- **Apple Music embed**: Embed an album, artist, or playlist
- **osu! profile**: Show rank, play count, and accuracy
- **Minecraft profile**: Display username, skin, cape, and Hypixel stats
- **Steam profile**: Show username, avatar, and level
- **GitHub repository**: Display owner, repo name, stars, and description
- **GitHub profile**: Show avatar, repository count, and followers
- **Twitter/X profile**: Display avatar, username, followers, and bio
- **Telegram channel/group**: Show name, avatar, and description
