Miwa.lol allows you to customize the font used on your profile. You can choose from a variety of fonts to match your style.

## Available Fonts

- Outfit
- Nunito
- Roboto
- Poppins
- Lacquer
- Fira Mono
- Lemon Milk
- Minecraft
- Noto Serif
- Noto Serif JP

## Custom Font <Badge type="note" text="Premium feature" />

**Premium users can upload their own custom font without needing to ask us to add it.** To upload a custom font, simply click the upload button next to the options.
