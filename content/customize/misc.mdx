This section includes additional options that don't fit into the other categories.

## Options

### Use Discord avatar

When enabled, your Discord avatar will replace your page avatar. Only available if you have connected your Discord account.

### Volume control

When enabled, a volume slider will appear on your page, allowing your visitors to adjust the volume of your audio or video background.

### Page enter text <Badge type="note" text="Premium feature" />

You can set a custom message that will be displayed when visitors enter your page.

<Aside type="info">
  This option will only be visible on your profile if you have an audio file or background video set.
</Aside>
