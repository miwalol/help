Miwa.lol Premium is available as a lifetime payment of €4.99. This means that you pay once and get access to all the Premium features forever.

To purchase Miwa.lol Premium, go to https://miwa.lol/pricing and click on "Purchase Premium". You will be redirected to a secure payment page where you can enter your payment details.

**Accepted Payment Methods:**
- Credit/Debit Cards
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Revolut Pay

## Features

- **Layouts**: Change the layout of your Miwa.lol page to suit your style
- **Privacy settings**: Hide your views count, likes, join date and more
- **Page enter text**: Customize the text that appears when someone enters your page
- **Cursor effects**: Add fun cursor effects to your Miwa.lol page
- **Typewriter Description**: Add a typewriter effect to your description, making it more engaging
- **Unlimited links**: Add as many links as you want to your page, whereas free users are limited to 5 links
- **More cards**: Add more cards to your page, with free users limited to 2 cards
- **Custom font**: Choose a custom font of your choice, instead of the default fonts available to free users
- **More audios**: Add more audio tracks to your page, with free users limited to 2 audio tracks

## Refund Policy

As explained in our [Terms of Service](https://miwa.lol/terms), we **do not offer refunds for Miwa.lol Premium**. Once you purchase Premium, you will have access to all the features for life.
We can only offer refunds in the case of a double charge or if your account was hacked and you didn't authorize the purchase.
