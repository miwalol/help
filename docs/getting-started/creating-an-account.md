To create an account, visit the [Miwa.lol website](https://miwa.lol) and click the "**Register for free**" button in the top right corner.

After registering, you'll receive a confirmation email with a link to verify your email address. Click the link to complete the verification process and activate your account.

## Onboarding Process

Once you've verified your email and logged in, a quick onboarding process will guide you through getting started with Miwa.lol. This process only takes a moment and includes a few questions to help us understand our users' needs and preferences, which enables us to improve Miwa.lol’s services for everyone.
