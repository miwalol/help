Miwa.lol allows you to customize the font used on your profile. You can choose from a variety of fonts to match your style.

## Available Fonts

- Nunito
- Roboto
- Poppins
- Lacquer
- Fira Mono
- Lemon Milk
- Minecraft
- Noto Serif
- Noto Serif JP

## Custom Font <Badge type="note" text="Premium feature" />

Premium users can upload their own custom font without needing to ask us to add it. To upload a custom font, simply click the upload button next to the search input.

![](./upload-font.png)

<Aside type="note">
  We support most common font formats: `ttf`, `otf`, `woff`, `woff2`, `eot` and `svg`.
</Aside>
