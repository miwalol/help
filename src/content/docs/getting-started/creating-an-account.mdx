## Registering

**You can either register with an email address, or use your Discord or Google account to sign up.**

### With an Email Address

If you choose to register with an email address, fill in the required fields, including your username, email address, and password. Make sure to read and agree to our [Terms of Service](https://miwa.lol/terms) before clicking the "**Register**" button.

<LinkCard
  title="Username, Display Name... What's the difference?" href="/account/username/"
  description="Learn about the differences between username, display name, and other profile fields."
/>

<Aside type="caution">
  Make sure to choose a strong password that is at least 12 characters long and includes a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
</Aside>

You'll need to verify your email before you can log in. Check your inbox for a verification email from Miwa.lol and click the link to verify your account.

### With Discord or Google

**If you prefer to use your Discord or Google account, simply click the corresponding button on the registration page.** You'll be redirected to the respective platform to authorize Miwa.lol to access your basic profile information. After authorizing, you'll be redirected back to Miwa.lol, and your account will be created automatically.

## Onboarding

Once you've verified your email and logged in, a quick onboarding process will guide you through getting started with Miwa.lol. This process only takes a moment and includes a few questions to help us understand our users' needs and preferences, which enables us to improve Miwa.lol's services for everyone.
